A Tribute to Massive Attack

Bio

Massive Attack is a British group formed in 1988 in Bristol, consisting of Robert "3D" Del Naja, Grant "Daddy G" Marshall and formerly Andy "Mushroom" Vowles ("Mush").

From their inception, Massive Attack quickly became pioneers of the trip-hop genre (though the bandmates have said in numerous interviews that they hate the term 'trip-hop'). People called Massive Attack's music 'trip-hop' because their musical style is very atmospheric, making a lot of use of bass and distorted instrumentals, which makes the listener feel like they are taking a trip through bass-infused electronic instrumentals reminiscent of hip-hop's. Massive Attack is also known for their sophiscated, world-weary lyrics - another parallel with hip-hop.
